About Me

I'm Jylah McKinney — a faith-driven photographer passionate about capturing moments that reflect light, love, and life. Each photo I take is a way to tell a story and share the beauty of God's creation. Whether I'm shooting portraits, videos, or meaningful candid moments, I aim to create work that inspires and uplifts through my own relationship with Christ.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring new cultures, kayaking, or engaging with my local community. Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio — I hope it brings you peace and joy.
